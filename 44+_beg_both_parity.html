Note:
  • PLL parity is only for even numbered cubes and not for odd nubered cubes
  • r, r', l, l' refers to the slice layer of parity
  • * refers to turn 2 layers at once for 4x4 and 3 layers at once for 6x6



OLL Parity

PLL Parity

Beginner Parity Algorithms



Parities are common in our solves(Almost 75% of our solves). There is 50% chance of OLL or PLL parity only and 25% chance of both the parities together.

PLL parity is only for even numbered cubes and not for odd nubered cubes. r, r', l, l' refers to the slice layer of parity. * refers to turn 2 layers at once for 4x4 and 3 layers at once for 6x6

Learn PLL Beginner PLL(3 steps), Advanced PLL(2 steps), Master PLL(1 step).

Learn OLL Beginner OLL(3 steps), Advanced OLL(2 steps),






OLL Parity



r U2 x r U2 r U2 r' U2 l U2 r' U2 r U2 r' U2 r' U2 x' r l' U2




PLL Parity



*Uw2 *Rw2 U2 r2 U2 *Rw2 *Uw2
